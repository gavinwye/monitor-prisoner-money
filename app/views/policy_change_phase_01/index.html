Service change from November 2nd

A policy change on November 2nd will mean senders have to 'Send money to someone in prison' using a debit card.

They will no longer be able to send money by bank transfer or send in cash, cheques or postal orders by post.

If they don't have a bank account, we're encouraging them to set one up. If they can't use this service, they can apply to the governor for an 'exemption' allowing them to still post money in.
